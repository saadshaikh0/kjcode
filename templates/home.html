Rules

  • For each test case passed you will be given 1 point.
  • Competitors will be ranked on the basis of no. of points they earn.
  • The person with the highest points will be declared the winner.
  • If two people have the same points than they will be judged on the basis of time.

  • Any malicious activity like cheating or copying will not be tolerated and may cause you disqualification.
